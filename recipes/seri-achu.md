# Achu by Seri

**Prep time:** 15 min | **Cook time:** 30 min | **Serves:** 4

## Ingredients
- Achu cocoyams, palm oil, spices, smoked fish, njamajama (huckleberry leaves)

## Steps
1. Boil and pound cocoyams into Achu paste
2. Prepare yellow soup with palm oil and spices
3. Serve paste with soup and greens
